Privacy Policy

Welcome to britishbookpublishers.co.uk. Your privacy is of utmost importance to us, and we are fully committed to safeguarding your personal information. Our Privacy Policy is frequently reviewed and updated to ensure compliance with current practices and legal requirements, and it operates under the Terms and Conditions applicable to our website.

In this Privacy Policy, the terms "we," "us," "our," and "service" refer to the britishbookpublishers.co.uk website, located at www.britishbookpublishers.co.uk. By accessing our website, you agree to the terms set out in this Privacy Policy, which governs the collection, sharing, and disclosure of your data during your visit.

The information we gather during your visit is used to improve our services and enhance your user experience. By using our website, you consent to the collection and utilisation of data as outlined in this policy.

Information Collection and Use

britishbookpublishers.co.uk collects various types of information to enhance the services we provide. The following categories of information may be gathered:

Personal Data

We may collect and store your Personal Data, which includes but is not limited to:

  • Your full name
  • Your email address
  • Your phone number
  • Your complete address
  • Cookies and Usage Data

britishbookpublishers.co.uk may gather Personal Data through direct interactions, newsletters, marketing or promotional materials, and other channels. While you can choose not to provide certain information, this may affect the range of services we are able to offer.

Usage Data

When you visit britishbookpublishers.co.uk, your browser may automatically collect Usage Data, which may include:

  • Your device’s IP address
  • The date and time of your visit
  • The duration of your visit and the pages viewed
  • Other diagnostic data

Cookies Data

We utilise cookies and similar technologies, such as beacons, tags, and scripts, to analyse your interactions with our website. You have the option to disable cookies through your browser settings if you prefer to prevent this type of data collection. However, please note that doing so may restrict access to certain features of our service.

britishbookpublishers.co.uk may collect the following:

  • Session Cookies
  • Preference Cookies
  • Security Cookies
  • Advertising Cookies

Other Data

Additionally, britishbookpublishers.co.uk may gather other information necessary to provide our services. This may include data such as:

  • Age, gender, place of birth, passport details, citizenship
  • Registration, address, contact information
  • Educational records, qualifications, professional training
  • Employment agreements, non-disclosure agreements (NDAs)
  • Marital status, social security number, taxpayer identification
  • Office location, and any other mandatory information

Purpose of Data Collection

britishbookpublishers.co.uk collects data for several reasons, including:

  • Enhancing the quality of our services
  • Providing updates regarding service changes
  • Improving customer support
  • Conducting detailed analysis to enrich the user experience
  • Monitoring service usage
  • Identifying and addressing technical issues
  • Meeting the objectives outlined in this Privacy Policy
  • Ensuring compliance with service agreements and informing users about terms and conditions
  • Maintaining records related to billing and collections
  • Updating you on your account or subscription status
  • Sharing news, special offers, and other relevant information about our services
  • Fulfilling any additional purposes with your consent

Data Storage

britishbookpublishers.co.uk will retain your data only for as long as necessary to achieve the purposes outlined in this Privacy Policy. We ensure secure storage in compliance with legal requirements and regulations.

Data Transfer

We employ strict security protocols to ensure that your data is handled securely and not transferred without your consent. In cases like mergers or third-party collaborations, robust measures are applied.

Disclosure of Data

Law Enforcement

We may be legally required to disclose your personal information to authorities.

Mergers and Business Acquisitions

In the event of a merger or asset sale, your personal data may be transferred.

Other Situations

  • Subsidiaries and affiliates
  • Contractors or third-party service providers
  • With your consent, when necessary

Data Security

We prioritise the highest security measures for collection, storage, and transfer of your data.

Third-Party Services

We may engage trusted third-party companies for optimisation, analytics, and payment processing, ensuring only essential data is shared.

Age of Consent

We collect data only from individuals of legal consent age. Any data found from minors will be deleted immediately.

Amendments and Updates
