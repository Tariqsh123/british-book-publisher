Frequently Asked Questions

The audiobook production cost in the UK typically ranges between £1,500 and £5,000+, depending on your book’s length, narrator's experience, and production complexity. Most publishers price by the “finished hour,” averaging £200–£400 per hour. When you transform your book into an audiobook, investing in quality narration and editing directly impacts listener satisfaction and long-term sales!

The process for audiobook production in England typically takes 6 to 12 weeks. Although timelines vary depending on the book’s length and the narrator's availability. Casting and narrator selection take 1–2 weeks, recording spans approximately 2–4 weeks, and editing or mastering takes an additional 2–3 weeks. Distribution setup and audiobook publishing preparation can take an additional 1–2 weeks.

Selecting the perfect voice artist in Britain is crucial. Through our audiobook narration services, we match your book’s genre with the right vocal tone, accent, and style. We consider key factors such as age suitability, emotional range, and whether your story needs a single narrator or character voices. Authors review voice auditions before final approval, ensuring the chosen narrator aligns with your creative vision.

ACX publishing UK refers to the Audiobook Creation Exchange, the official platform for Audible publishing services. It connects rights holders with narrators and manages audiobook submissions to Audible, iTunes, and Amazon. ACX requires specific audio quality and mastering standards. Authors can choose exclusive (higher royalties) or non-exclusive (wider distribution) contracts.

Yes, you can. Many authors prefer to narrate their own work using our audiobook recording services UK. It adds authenticity, especially for non-fiction titles. However, professional production services are often needed to meet technical and performance standards. Self-narration can be challenging due to pacing, clarity, and stamina. We offer coaching, studio access, and editing support to help authors deliver broadcast-quality narration.

Our British audiobook distribution network ensures your title appears on all major platforms, including Audible, iTunes/Apple Books, Google Play, Kobo, and various niche audiobook services. When you publish an audiobook in the UK, we handle platform formatting, metadata optimisation, and file submissions. You can choose exclusive distribution for higher royalties or wide distribution for global reach.

Royalty structures in audiobook publishing vary by platform. Through Audible publishing services, authors earn up to 40% royalties on exclusive contracts and around 25% on non-exclusive ones. Other platforms typically pay between 25% and 30%. We help authors choose the most profitable option based on their goals, manage royalty tracking, and provide transparent reports.
